About Cake Factory

Welcome to Cake Factory, where every slice tells a story! Founded in Helsinki in 2024, we are passionate about crafting exquisite cakes that celebrate life's sweetest moments. At Cake Factory, we believe in the magic of homemade recipes, quality ingredients, and innovative design.

Our journey began with Jafar, Jasper, Jonne & Miro, whose love for baking and artistry turned into a flourishing local business. Today, Cake Factory is a cherished destination for cake enthusiasts, offering a diverse range of flavors from classic vanilla to exotic passion fruit. Each cake is a masterpiece, adorned with precision and care, and designed to not only look spectacular but taste heavenly.

At Cake Factory, we are committed to sustainability and community involvement. We source our ingredients from local suppliers to ensure freshness and support our community. Our team of talented bakers and decorators work tirelessly to create cakes that not only meet, but exceed your expectations.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or just a Tuesday, Cake Factory is here to make your day unforgettable. Join us in our sweet adventure and discover why every celebration deserves a Cake Factory cake!

Visit us or order online, and let us add a sprinkle of joy to your next occasion!
